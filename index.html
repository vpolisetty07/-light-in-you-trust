Our Mission

We believe every person deserves care, dignity, and a future.

Light in You Trust exists to fill the quiet gaps the world overlooks — a grandmother without a hot meal, a child without school fees, a village without a doctor. We show up. We do the work. We stay.

What We Do

Three programs. Real change.

Medical Camps

Free health camps in under-served villages and slums — check-ups, medicines, eye care, and specialist consultations. Brought to the doorstep, no paperwork required.

  • 40+ camps held
  • 12,000+ patients seen

Food for the Elderly

Hot, nutritious meals delivered to elders who live alone, are home-bound, or have been left behind. Because no one should grow old hungry, or unseen.

  • 800+ meals weekly
  • 5 neighborhoods served

Education for Single-Parent Children

School fees, books, uniforms, and mentorship for children being raised by one parent. We help them stay in school — and dream bigger than their circumstances.

  • 120+ students supported
  • 100% retention rate

Our Story

It started with one meal, one neighbor.

Light in You Trust was born the day a few friends realized the elderly woman down the street hadn't eaten a proper meal in days. One tiffin became ten. Ten became a kitchen. The kitchen became a movement.

Today, we run medical camps, deliver thousands of meals, and put children through school — not because it is easy, but because it is right. Every rupee is accounted for. Every program is run by people who care.

— The Light in You Trust Family

“When you light a lamp for someone else, it also brightens your own path.”
