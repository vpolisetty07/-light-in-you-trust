What We Do

Three programs. Real lives.

Each program is run by volunteers, accounted for line-by-line, and designed to meet a single, dignified human need. We do a small number of things, and we do them with patience.

Health, brought to the doorstep.

Free health check-ups, medicines, eye care, and specialist consultations in under-served villages and urban neighbourhoods. No paperwork. No payment. No questions. Just a doctor, and time.

Our Story

It started with one meal, one neighbour.

Light in You Trust began the day a few friends realised the elderly woman down the street hadn't eaten a proper meal in days. One tiffin became ten. Ten became a kitchen. The kitchen, in time, became something larger than any of us had planned for.

Today we run free medical camps for villages that wouldn't otherwise see a doctor; we deliver thousands of meals each month to elders who live alone; and we fund the schooling of children whose single parent is doing everything they can on what is never enough.

We are not a large institution. We do not aspire to be one. We aspire, instead, to be careful — with the rupees we are given and with the lives we are entrusted to. Every program is run by people who know the people they serve.

— The Light in You Trust family

Frequently Asked

Your questions, answered.

Are donations to Light in You Trust tax-deductible?

Yes. Light in You Trust is registered under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, so every donation is eligible for tax deduction under Indian law. We issue an official 80G receipt by email within 24 hours of receiving your contribution.

How can I donate?

The simplest way is the Donate button above — it opens a Razorpay payment page that accepts cards, UPI, NetBanking, and wallets. You can also pay directly to the UPI ID lightinyou@upi. For corporate CSR or large institutional donations, please email donate@lightinyoutrust.org and we will guide you through the documentation.

Where does Light in You Trust operate?

We are based in India and run our medical camps, elderly meal programs, and child education sponsorships across multiple under-resourced neighbourhoods. We expand to new locations only as funding allows and only when local volunteers are in place.

How can I volunteer?

Volunteers are the heart of what we do. You can help cook and deliver meals, organise medical camps, mentor sponsored children, manage events, or contribute professional skills — design, accounting, legal, medical. Sign up via the contact form below.

What percentage of my donation reaches beneficiaries?

Over 95% of every rupee donated reaches our beneficiaries directly. We are volunteer-run, which keeps overheads exceptionally low, and we publish detailed annual transparency reports listing every program expense.

Can I sponsor a specific child or elder?

Yes. We offer one-to-one sponsorship programs for both children's education and elderly meal delivery. You will receive periodic updates on the person you sponsor. Email hello@lightinyoutrust.org to be matched.
