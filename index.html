Our Mission

Every person deserves care, dignity, and a future.

Light in You Trust exists to fill the quiet gaps the world overlooks — a grandmother without a hot meal, a child without school fees, a village without a doctor. We show up. We do the work. We stay.

What We Do

Three programs. Real change.

Free Medical Camps

Free health check-ups, medicines, eye care, and specialist consultations in under-served villages and urban slums. Brought to your doorstep — no paperwork, no payment, no questions.

  • 40+ camps held
  • 12,000+ patients seen

Meals for the Elderly

Hot, nutritious daily meals delivered to elderly Indians who live alone, are home-bound, or have been left behind by their families. Because no one should grow old hungry — or unseen.

  • 800+ meals weekly
  • 5 neighborhoods served

Education for Children of Single Parents

School fees, books, uniforms, and one-on-one mentorship for children being raised by a single parent. We help them stay in school — and dream bigger than their circumstances.

  • 120+ students supported
  • 100% retention rate

Our Story

It started with one meal, one neighbor.

Light in You Trust was born the day a few friends realized the elderly woman down the street hadn't eaten a proper meal in days. One tiffin became ten. Ten became a kitchen. The kitchen became a movement.

Today, we run free medical camps, deliver thousands of meals to the elderly, and sponsor the education of children raised by single parents — not because it is easy, but because it is right. Every rupee is accounted for. Every program is run by people who care.

— The Light in You Trust Family

When you light a lamp for someone else, it also brightens your own path.
— A guiding principle

Frequently Asked

Your questions, answered.

How can I donate to Light in You Trust?

You can donate via UPI, NEFT/IMPS bank transfer, or contact us for international wire details. Full account details are listed in the Donate section above. For corporate CSR or large donations, please email donate@lightinyoutrust.org and we'll handle the rest.

Where does Light in You Trust operate?

We are based in India and run our medical camps, elderly meal programs, and child education sponsorships across multiple under-resourced neighborhoods. We are expanding to new locations as funding allows — partner with us to bring our programs to your community.

How can I volunteer with Light in You Trust?

Volunteers are the heart of what we do. You can help cook and deliver meals, organize medical camps, mentor sponsored children, manage events, or contribute professional skills (design, accounting, legal, medical). Sign up via the contact form on this page.

What percentage of my donation reaches beneficiaries?

Over 95% of every rupee donated reaches our beneficiaries directly. We are volunteer-run, which keeps overheads exceptionally low, and we publish detailed annual transparency reports listing every program expense.

Can I sponsor a specific child or elder?

Yes. We offer one-on-one sponsorship programs for both children's education and elderly meal delivery. You'll receive periodic updates on the person you sponsor. Email hello@lightinyoutrust.org to be matched.
